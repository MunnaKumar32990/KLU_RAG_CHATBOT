Eligibility Criteria

B.Tech:
- 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
- Minimum qualifying marks as per university norms.
- Valid entrance exam score (KLEEE/JEE/EAMCET).

MBA:
- Bachelor's degree in any discipline.
- Minimum aggregate marks as per norms.
- Valid KLMAT/CAT/MAT/XAT score.

Other UG Programs:
- 10+2 in relevant stream with required minimum marks.
