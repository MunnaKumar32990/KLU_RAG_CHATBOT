KL University Admission Process

1. Candidates must apply online through the official KL University admissions portal.
2. Admission to B.Tech programs is based on KLEEE / JEE Main / AP EAMCET scores.
3. MBA admissions are based on KLMAT / CAT / MAT / XAT scores followed by interview.
4. Applicants must upload required documents and pay the application fee online.
5. After qualifying, students attend counseling and complete fee payment to confirm admission.
6. Academic sessions begin as per university academic calendar.
